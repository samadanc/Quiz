Title: Time/Date Update Notification

Primary Actor: Instructor

Stakeholders and Interests:

Instructor: wants to notify students on a time/date change of the quiz

Student: wants update or notifcation of time/date changes made to quiz

Technician: wants to keep track of those changes

Precondition: User with administrative rights logs in to make changes.

Trigger: Instructor makes changes to a quiz time/date.

Main Scenario:

  1. Instructor decides that the time/date of the quiz is to be changed or extended.
  2. Instructor selects quiz for changes
  3. Instructor selects students registered under quiz item
  4. Instructor confirms date/time changes
  5. Instructor updates the time/date of the quiz
  6. SuD logs those changes into the system.
  7. Instructor repeats steps 1-4 if another quiz requires changes
  8. SuD records all changes and sends notification
  9. Students are notified of changes made by Instructor

Extensions:

7a. Update Sent

7a1. SuD sends update to student listed under quiz item only

8a. Notification time/date change

8a1. SuD displays time/date notifcation upon Student Login.
