Title: Quiz Time Remaining

Primary Actor:Student

Stakeholders and Interests::

Student - While taking the quiz, student should be prompted by the time left for the quiz

Instructor - Can modify the number of minutes to notify the student, which are left for the quiz

Precondition: Instructor should be logged in and should already have a set quiz

Trigger: Need of remainder for student

Main Scenario:

  1. Instructor logs into the quiz
  2. SuD lists actions to the Instructor and he follows them
  3. SuD performs the quiz and shows the remaining time left at the last five minutes(if 5 mins is assigned).
  4. Instructor verifies it

Extensions:

1a. Instructor wants to add notification at the last minute

1a1. SuD displays the last minute notification at the end of the quiz
