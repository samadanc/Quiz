Title: Quiz Modification

Primary Actor: Instructor

Stakeholders and Interests::

Instructor - can modify the questions, quiz pattren and weightage of the questions of an already existed quiz

Precondition: Instructor should be logged in and should already have a set quiz

Trigger: Change in course content

Main Scenario:

  1. Instructor selects the quiz he wants to modify
  2. SuD lists actions to the Instructor that he can take to respective quiz

Extensions:

1a. Instructor wants to add a new question to pre-exisiting quiz

1a1. SuD generates the contant creation form with the fileds to put add new question contant in it
