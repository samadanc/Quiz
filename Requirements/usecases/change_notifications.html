Title: Change Notifications

Primary Actor: Student

Stakeholders and Interests:

Student: Wants to change quiz notifications

Instructor: Wants to provide quiz notifications

Technician: Wants log of notifications given to Students

Precondition: Notifications are enabled for Student

Trigger: Student wants to change notifications

Main Scenario:

  1. Student decides to update notifications
  2. Student selects to change quiz notifications
  3. SuD initiates menu for student to change when notifications are received
  4. Student changes notification time
  5. SuD updates the student's quiz notification time
  6. SuD notifies student that changes are made

Extensions:

4a. Student can choose what time the notification appears

4a1. Student manually chooses time for notification

4a2. SuD confirms and applies changes
