Title: Email Professor with Questions

Primary Actor: Student

Stakeholders and Interests:

Student: Wants to clarify details about the quiz results after the quiz has been sumbitted

Instructor: Wants feedback on quiz, to be able to solve issues with marking.

Precondition: Student has finished quiz and has recieved a grade.

Main Scenario:

  1. Student logs in to their class quiz page
  2. Student takes quiz
  3. Student has submitted quiz solution
  4. SuD calculates quiz mark
  5. SuD presents Student with quiz mark
  6. Student selects option to contact professor
  7. SuD opens a GUI box to compose an email, with both emails of the prof and of the student already in their respective places.

Extensions:

1a. Send email to someone other than the professor.

1a1. Student deletes the 'to' area of the email.

1a2. Student writes in the new recipient's name.
