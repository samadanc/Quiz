Title: Export Grade

Primary Actor: Instructor

Stakeholders and Interests:

Instructor: wants to export student's grades to CSV

Tech: wants operations to be traceable

Precondition: Student's grades have been saved

Trigger: Instructor wants to save the quiz grade for data analysis.

Main Scenario:

  1. Instructor logs in the grades page.
  2. Instructor clicks "Export" button.
  3. Export Grade Items For ALL users/Selections.
  4. Choose User Details from Last Name/First Name/Course Mail/ Section Membership.
  5. Choose all/specific grades to export.
  6. Instructor clicks Export to CSV/Excel.

Extensions:

1a.Instructor cannot log in the grades page.

1a1. Instructor input correct password.

6a.SuD failure Export to CSV/Excel.

6a1. Instructor select grades to export.
