Title: Save Answers

Primary Actor: Student

Stakeholders and Interests:

Student: wants to save their answers

Tech: wants log of saved answers

Precondition: Student is currently taking the quiz and has answered questions.

Trigger: Student requests to save their answers.

Main Scenario:

1. Student selects option to save answers

2. SuD saves all answers

3. SuD logs the saved answers and the time at which they were saved

4. SuD says answers saved

5. Student closes the quiz

6. Student returns to the quiz

7. SuD displays the quiz with all saved answers unchanged

Extensions:

2a. SuD fails to save answers

2a1. SuD says failed to save answers
