Title: Excused Absence

Primary Actor: Student

Stakeholders and Interests:

Student: wants the quiz grade to be excused

Instructor: wants valid proof of absence

Precondition: Submission time for quiz has passed.

Main Scenario:

  1. Student notifies instructor that they missed the quiz
  2. Instructor receives notification
  3. Instructor asks for proof for missing quiz
  4. Student provides proof
  5. Instructor receives and approves of proof
  6. Instructor gives an excused for quiz
  7. Instructor notifies student that the mark change has been made
  8. Instructor modifies weight of grade to reflect the missed assignment

Extensions:

4a. Student fails to provide proof

4a1. Instructor leaves the mark for quiz as 0

5a. Ivalid proof is provided

5a1. Instructor can request proper submission of proof for absence

5a2. Instructor can re-review students submission of proof
