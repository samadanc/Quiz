Title: System maintiance

Primary Actor: Users and Technician

Stakeholders and Interests:

Users - get problems when they use system,and report to Technician.

Technician - receive the problem reports and fix that.

Main Scenario:

  1. Users get problems and click a link jumping to a reporting page.
  2. Users describe the problems, and then, submit it.
  3. Technician receives the reporting and have a quick inspection to confirm.
  4. Technician will fix the problems after a confirmation of bug .

Extensions:

1a.Technician does not find any problems after a inspection

1a1.The reason of problem existence may caused by users' incorrectly operation

1a2.System will lead users to a guidance page.
