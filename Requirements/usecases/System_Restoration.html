Title: System Restoration

Primary Actor: Student

Stakeholders and Interests:

Student: wants to continue the quiz after system crashing

Instructor: wants student to finish quiz successfully

Tech: wants to get feedback from the crash

Precondition: Student logged in to quiz page and system is down .

Main Scenario:

  1. Student log in their account
  2. Student choose quiz and start to answer quiz
  3. the system is down by any unredictable issues
  4. Student re-log in their account and choose the same quiz
  5. System detects student has an incompleted quiz which is not submitted
  6. System asks whether to continue
  7. Student choose to continue previous quiz and finish it or choose to submit directly
  8. Student confirms quiz submission
