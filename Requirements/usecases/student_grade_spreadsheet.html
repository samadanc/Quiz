Title: Export Student Grade to Spreadsheet

Primary Actor: Student

Stakeholders and Interests:

Student: wants to export their grades to the spread sheet

Computer Technician: Would like the operations to be traceable and working correctly

Precondition: Student's grades must be saved before continuation

Trigger: Student wants to save their grades to spreadsheet for personal use and to forecast future performance in a more concise way.

Main Scenario:

  1. Student logs in to their own account with their login username/password.
  2. Student clicks “Grades” button.
  3. Student clicks “Save Grades” button.
  4. Export Grade Items For all users/courses.
  5. Choose the grades for each course that you want specifically viewed on the spreadsheet to keep track of.
  6. Student clicks the button “Transport” that automatically transfers to spreadsheet.

Extensions:

1a.Student encounters a system failure during logging in to the grades page.

1a1.Web server problem, please wait and try another time.

2a.Students cannot log into the grades page to view grades.

2a2.INCORRECT PASSWORD entered.
