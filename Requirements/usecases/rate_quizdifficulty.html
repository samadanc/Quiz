Title:Rate Quiz Difficulty

Primary Actor:Student

Stakeholders and Interests:

Student: want to rate the difficulty of the selected quiz after they completed this quiz

Instructor: want students to give difficlity rates on each quiz

Precondition: Students have completed the quiz which they want to rate and received their grades.

Trigger: rating section of the selected quiz is available.

Main Scenario:

  1. Student log in to quiz website
  2. SuD displays the list of completed quizzes
  3. student choose which quiz to rate
  4. SuD lists all actions that student can take of the selected quiz
  5. student clicks the "Rate Difficulty" button
  6. student gives the score of difficulty out of 5
  7. student clicks "Submit" to submit the rate
  8. student repeats from step 3 to 7 as desired

Extensions:

2.a There is no completed quiz

2.a.1 student cannot do any modification to any quiz

6.a student didn't complete this rate

6.a.1 student drops the rating session
