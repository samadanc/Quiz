Title: Passing Grade

Primary Actor: Student

Stakeholders and Interests:

Student: wants their grade classified as a pass or fail

Tech: wants record of passes and fails

Precondition: Quiz can be failed.

Trigger: Student has submitted a quiz.

Main Scenario:

  1. Student submits quiz solution.
  2. SuD confirms quiz submission.
  3. SuD calculates quiz grade.
  4. SuD presents student with grade and if they failed or passed.
  5. SuD records quiz as passed or failed.

Extensions:

2a. Submission rejected

2a1. SuD says quiz incomplete

2a2. Student forces incomplete submission
