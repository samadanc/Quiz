Title:Arrange a new quiz

Primary Actor: Instructor

Stakeholders and Interests:

Instructor: Arrange a new quiz if the original one is canceled

Students: Email instructor and provides reasons for arranging a new quiz

Precondition: User with instructor privileges logged in already.

Main Scenario:

  1. SuD email the instructor and explain the reason why ask for arranging a new quiz time
  2. Instructor selects to agree or disagree
  3. Instructor provides some times for SuD to select
  4. SuD selects the new quiz time
  5. Instructor confirms and arranges the new quiz time successfully

Extensions:

3a.Instructor disagree the request:

3a1. SuD can not be arranged a new quiz time

4a.SuD has a time conflict with the time instructor provided:

4a1. SuD should email instructor to arrange another quiz time
