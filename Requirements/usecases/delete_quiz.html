Title: Delete Quiz

Primary Actor: Instructor

Stakeholders and Interests:

Instructor: Delete an online quiz

Precondition: User with instructor privileges logged in already.

Main Scenario:

  1. Instructor right selects on an online quiz
  2. SuD presents a list of options
  3. Instructor selects the delete option
  4. SuD asks Instructor to confirm delete
  5. Instructor selects "Yes" confirmation
  6. SuD deletes the quiz

Extensions:

4a. Instructor changes his mind on deleting Quiz

4a1. Insrtructor selects "No" confirmation

4a2. SuD does not delete the quiz
