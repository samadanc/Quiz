Title: Multiple Attempt Quiz

Primary Actor: Student

Stakeholders and Interests:

Teacher: Wants to insure comprehension of material and minimize ability for cheating.

Precondition: Student starts a quiz.

Main Scenario:

1. SuD randomly selects from the list of versions of a question that do not have a “Already Presented” flag on them unless all versions are found to have this flag.

2. SuD Flags this question as having been presented.

3. SuD Presents the question to the student

4. Student answers question and proceeds to next question.

5. Student continues to work on quiz and repeats Step 1-4 until all questions are completed.

6. Student submits quiz

7. SuD confirms quiz submission

8. Student may reopen quiz and repeat steps 1-7 until either maximum number of attempts is reached or the student is content with their grade.
