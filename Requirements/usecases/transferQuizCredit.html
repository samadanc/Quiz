Title: Transfer a quiz credit

Primary Actor: Instructor

Stakeholders and Interests:

Student: Notify instructor with a valid reason and explanation to transfer a quiz credit to final

Instructor: Verify the reason and transfer the credit upon validation

IT assistant: Transfer the quiz credit to final upon instructor's wish

Precondition: Student needs to submit proper documents.

Main Scenario:

  1. Student will collect doctor's prescription for medical reason
  2. Student need to collect other document for other than medical reason
  3. Student will request the instructor to transer the credit
  4. SuD will collect all the documents from the student
  5. Instructor will decide for the quiz to be credited or alterd
  6. SuD will inform student about the final decision

Extensions:

1a.Student do not have any medical issue:

1a1. Student will collect proper documents to be submitted

2a.Student failed to collect documents:

2a1. Instructor may provide additionnal time

2a2. Instructor may refuse the credit transfer request

5a.Student need to attend altered quiz:

5a1. Failure to attend the altered quiz score will be a zero
