Title:Tick Question

Primary Actor: Student

Stakeholders and Interests:

Student: wants the specific question(s) be explained in class

Instructor: wants to know which quiz question(s) should be explained in class

Precondition: Student starts a quiz.

Main Scenario:

  1. SuD presents the special quiz instruction("If you don't understand or not sure about the answer of the question(s), you can tick the box beside the corresponding question(s)") in the beginning of the quiz.
  2. SuD presents the quiz questions with a small box beside each of them.
  3. Student works on the quiz questions
  4. Student finds out a question that he/she does not understand or not sure how to answer.
  5. Student decides to click on the box to tick the question.
  6. Student continue works on quiz and may repeats 4-5 as desired.
  7. Student submits quiz solution.
  8. SuD confirms quiz submission and highlight the ticked question.

Extensions:

5a. Untick Quiz

5a1. Student finds out the answer and understands it completely

5a2. Student click on the ticked box to untick the question.
